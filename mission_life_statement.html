Life Mission Statement Page

What is a Life Mission Statment?

A Life Mission Statment is a "motto" for you to follow in your life. It can reflect your life choices, reponsibilities, and biases.
It can be found in many places, like companies, big or small.
Many (like I) create life mission statments by jotting down their purposes, their values, and their passions.
My jotting session can be found on the upper-right side of the page.

My Life Mission Statement & Explanation

My statment is: Endure the hardships to give and get the best product.
How did I come up with this particular statement? Justification is in the box below:

While thinking about creating a life mission statment, I realized that my passions, values, and my purposes can have challenges, or hardships.
To overcome these hardships in my life, I had to endure them.
Similarly, to make the best out of your life / to feel a sense of fulfilment in your actions, you must get and give the best product/actions.
So in conclusion, I chose this statment because it describes my life and what I strive to do.
